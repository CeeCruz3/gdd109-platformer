# gdd109-endless-runner



ED: Factory Rush is a game where you play as a robot named ED, short for "Endless Dasher," going through a production test to see how far you can make it before running out of energy or crashing. You collect points of condensed light, as ED is solar powered and needs to constantly collect these or risk running out of power. Some obstacle ideas are a pipe that you have to jump on/over, a hurdle to duck under/jump over, and a wall that must be ducked under. Running drains your energy, and jumping and ducking cost a small amount of energy to do as well.
As the durability test continues, more obstacles are added and ED moves quicker, draining your energy faster, so make sure to collect any light you see to stay moving for as long as you can.

The current plan is to create the energy mechanic first, then work on obstacles, then difficulty. The visual elements of the game will be worked on near last, though I will work on geting ED's animations throughout next week, as well as Light pickups.
Most assets will be made by me, though I am unsure how well that'd pan out. Difficulty will be determined by time, and some obstacle patterns will be added as the difficulty progresses.
