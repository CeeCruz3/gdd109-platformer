# gdd109-endless-runner



This will be an endless runner game made for GDD109. While it is mostly barebones, the plan is to make a very simple game. The current idea is a factory dash, where you play as a robot, ED, short for "Endless Dasher," going through a production test to see how far you can make it before running out of energy or crashing. Main collectable will be points of condensed light, as ED is solar powered and needs to constantly collect these or risk running out of power. Current obstacle ideas are a pipe that you have to jump on/over, a hurdle to duck under/jump over, and an obstacle that must be kicked. Kicking and jumping cost a small bit of energy.
As the durability test continues, more obstacles are added and ED moves quicker, requiring you to duck, jump, and kick quicker, while making sure to collect any light you see to stay moving.
